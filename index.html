DETAIL:

Mark

Cooldown: 80 seconds

Range: 1600

ACTIVE: Throw a snowball at a long distance in the target direction, dealing 15-100(based on level) true damage to the first enemy unit hit, reveaking them for 3 seconds and granting you the ability to cast Dash for the next 3 second.


Mark will grant sight of Brush while passing through.


ATTENTION: only applicable in Aram mode

Dash

Cooldown: 0 seconds

Range: Global

ACTIVE: Dash to the target by Mark while being untagetable, dealing 15 - 100 (based on level) true damage to them upon arrival. Dashing to the target will reduce Mark's cool by 25%.


You lose the ability to cast Dash if the marked target dies or remoces the mark.


ATTENTION: only applicable in Aram mode

Heal

Cooldown: 240 seconds

Range: 850

Healsyour champion and the allied champion nearest to the cursor (prioritizes the most wounded allied champion if no allied champions are near the cursor) for 90-345(based on level), while also granting both of you 30% (bonus total) movement speed for 1 seconds.


Champions affected by Heal within 35 seconds cause subsequent Heal cast on them to retore 50% less health.

Ghost

Cooldown: 210 seconds

Range: self

Your champion ignores unit collision and gains 24%-48%(based on level) bonus movement speed for 10seconds, increased by 4-7 (based on level) seconds whenever scoring a takedown against an enemy champion.

Barrier

Cooldown: 180 seconds

Range: Self

Shields your champion for 115 - 455(based onlevel) for 2 seconds.

Exhaust

Cooldown: 210 seconds

Range: 650

Weakens the target enemy champion , slowing them by 30% and reducing their damage dealt by 40% for 3 seconds.

Clarity

Cooldown: 240 seconds

Range: 600

Restores 50% maximum mana to your champion and 25% maximum mana to nearby allied champion.


ATTENTION: only applicable in Aram mode

Flash

Cooldown: 300 seconds

Range: 400 (Smart cast)

Blinks your champion a short distance toward the target location.

Teleport

Cooldown: 360 seconds

Range: Global

After channeling for 4seconds, removes any homestart or homeguard buff and blinks your champion to target allied turret.


Upgrades to Unleashed Teleport at 14:00.

Smite

Cooldown: 15 seconds

Recharge: 90 seconds

Range: 500

Deals 450 true damage to the target medium / large monster, enemt minion, or pet.


Using Smite against a medium / large monster heals for 90 (+ 10% of your maximum health).

Cleanse

Cooldown: 210 seconds

Range: Self

Removes all disables (excluding Airborne, Nearsight, and Suppression) and summoner spell debuffs (excluding Mark) affecting your champion , as well as grating 65% tenacity for 3 seconds.

Ignite

Cooldown: 180 seconds

Range: 600
